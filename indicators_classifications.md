# Advanced Classification Framework

## Predictive Indicators
Attempt to forecast future movement.
- Divergence models
- Sentiment indicators
- Breadth thrusts
- Volatility compression systems

## Reactive Indicators
Respond after movement occurs.
- Moving averages
- Trend-following systems
- Breakout systems

## Leading Indicators
Potentially signal before price moves.
- RSI divergence
- Breadth divergence
- Volume accumulation

## Lagging Indicators
Confirm existing movement.
- SMA
- ADX
- MACD signal cross

---
